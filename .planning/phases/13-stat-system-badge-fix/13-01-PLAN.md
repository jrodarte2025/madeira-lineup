**Existing architecture:** stat types are centralized in `src/shared/constants.js` (POSITION_STATS, STAT_COLORS, STAT_LABELS) and `src/shared/summaryUtils.js` (STAT_ORDER, MVP_ABBREV). Every downstream consumer (StatBar, EventsFeed, LiveGameScreen badge, GameSummaryScreen, StatsTab, seasonUtils.computeSeasonDeltas) reads from these central maps and iterates events filtered by `e.type === "stat"`. Adding a new stat key is therefore a four-line change that automatically propagates end-to-end — no per-component edits needed.

**Badge bug:** `LiveGameScreen.jsx` line 846 filters stat events by `e.half === currentHalf`. After halftime, 1st-half stat counts vanish from the on-field badges. Jim's feedback #8 wants whole-game totals.
</context>
