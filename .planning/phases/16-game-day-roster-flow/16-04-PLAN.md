Remove the "sit player" / "make inactive" UI affordances from the saved-lineup-template builder (the `/lineup` tab, `MadeiraLineupPlanner.jsx`). Output: Code-deletion of the SIT button and inactive drop zone from `src/MadeiraLineupPlanner.jsx`, plus minor cleanup of `toggleInactive` callsites that no longer have UI surfaces.
</objective>
